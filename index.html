FAQ

Got questions? We have answers.

How do I register for the Thrive Rewards program?

Answer

If your location offers Thrive Rewards, you can register in the following ways:

  1. Download the Thrive App
  2. If your location offers Thrive Rewards, you will see an announcement pop-up letting you know the program has launched
  3. You can also register for the program by navigating to the rewards dashboard in the bottom navigation or by the opting in prompt in checkout
  4. Once you have activated your Thrive Rewards account, you can earn points on your mobile and kiosk orders

How does the Thrive Rewards program work?

Answer

  • To participate in the Thrive Rewards program, you first need to activate your rewards account in Thrive
  • You will earn points automatically for every mobile purchase unless a brand or location is excluded from the program
  • To earn points on kiosk orders, scan your Thrive Rewards digital card at the kiosk when checking out
  • For special offers to earn bonus points, check your rewards dashboard in the app or look out for email offers. Be sure to subscribe to email promotions & updates!
  • When you have collected enough points, select rewards you want to purchase and use towards your next mobile or kiosk order.
  • The more you spend, the faster you will accumulate your next reward!

What are the benefits of being a being a Thrive Rewards member?

Answer

  • Save more with every order!
  • Earn 10 points for every $1 you spend
  • Earn points and redeem rewards on your mobile & kiosk orders
  • Receive exclusive promotion emails to earn points faster
  • Get $1 off for every 500 points you earn

Is there a fee to be a Thrive Rewards member?

Answer

  • No. The program is absolutely free!

How can I earn points?

Answer

  • Earn points with every mobile and kiosk transaction when you scan your rewards card
  • Earn bonus points with exclusive offers in app and email
  • Points will only be earn on pre-taxes once any discounts or promotions have been applied

How (and where) can I redeem my points?

Answer

  • Use your points to purchase reward vouchers in the Thrive app
  • From there, you can either pre-purchase your rewards in the dashboard or directly within basket
  • For kiosk orders, you will have to first pre-purchase your voucher in order for it to show up on the kiosk when you scan your rewards card
  • For mobile orders, you can buy and apply rewards directly in your basket
  • Note: Loyalty may only be redeemed at specified locations or brands

How do I check my point balance?

Answer

  • You can check your Thrive Rewards balance in the the Thrive app in the Rewards dashboard

Can I redeem more than one reward at a time?

Answer

  • No. You can only apply one reward to a single order at a time

When will my balance be updated after I make a purchase or receive a special offer?

Answer

  • You should see your account updated within 24 hours

Will the points I collect expire?

Answer

  • Your points and account expire after 6 months of inactivity. In order to avoid losing your point balance please place an order or redeem a voucher within the 6 month time frame.

Will the rewards I purchased expire?

Answer

  • Rewards expire 30 days after purchase. In order to avoid losing a reward, please use it towards your order within 30 days.
